User settings

/factions settings chat [global|faction|focus]       üí¨ Changes which chat you're currently sending and receiving from.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `global` sends to everyone on the server, and receives both `global` and `faction` chats.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `faction` sends to only your faction members, and receives both `global` and `faction` chats.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `focus` sends to only your faction members, and receives only your `faction` chat.
/factions settings radar       üí¨ Toggles displaying the faction of the chunk you're currently standing inside on the hud.
/factions settings sound [all|warnings|factions|none]       üí¨ Changes what type of sounds the mod will play.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `all` plays all sounds.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `warnings` only plays warnings sounds (eg. placing a block inside another factions claim).
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `faction` only plays faction event sounds (eg. faction losing power).
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è `none` disables all the sounds.

Faction Management

Claiming

‚ùî Requirements: is at least ranked commander
/factions claim list       üí¨ Lists all your faction claims.
/factions claim add [<size>]       üí¨ Claims the chunk you're standing in, or a radius around it.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è The `size` parameter is limited to a max of 7 (15x15, 225 chunks).
/factions claim remove [<size>|all]       üí¨ Removes the claim on the chunk you're standing in, or a radius around it.
      ‚ÑπÔ∏è The `size` parameter is limited to a max of 7 (15x15, 225 chunks).
      :warning: The `all` parameter will remove all claims, be careful!
