Economy wiki - Commands

Information on commands n shit

/diamonds balance [Optional: player] - tells user how many diamonds the player has


/diamonds deposit - takes diamonds from your inventory and adds them to your account


/diamonds send [player] [int] - takes [int] diamonds form your account and adds them to [player]'s account


/diamonds top - tells user current rank along with who has the most diamonds and how many


/diamonds withdraw [int] - takes [int] diamonds from your account and puts them in your inventory in the most diamond blocks possible
